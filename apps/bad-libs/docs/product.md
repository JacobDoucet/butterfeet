# Bad Libs Product Notes

## MVP Scope

- Fully local Android app
- Bundled JSON story packs
- Core flow: Home -> Packs -> Stories -> Prompts -> Reveal -> Share
- No accounts, backend, ads, billing, or analytics in MVP

## Future Monetization Ideas

- Optional paid premium story packs
- One-time "remove ads" purchase (if ads are added later)
- Bundle packs into themed season drops

## Future Features

- Ads (post-MVP only)
- Premium packs
- AI-generated stories
- Multiplayer / pass-and-play modes
